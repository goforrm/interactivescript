Interesting... Generally, the lower your premium, the more risk your accepting personally. A lot of people don’t know that.

  • [If they want examples] If you hit someone driving a $60k truck and only have $25k property coverage, you’re responsible for the other $35k. With Farmers, we’ll build a policy to protect you from that risk.
  • On the other side, if you're hurt in an accident with a driver who has no insurance, you’ll need to use your policy. Cheaper carriers typically give the lowest limits possible and may exclude Uinsured Motorists coverage.
